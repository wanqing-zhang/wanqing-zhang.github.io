**About me**
Hi there, I'm Wanqing Zhang 👋
I am a Ph.D. candicate in Engineering Science and Mechanics at The Pennsylvania State Univeristy, where I conduct research on wearable electronics under the supervision of Prof. Huanyu Cheng.
