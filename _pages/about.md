**Hi there, I'm Wanqing Zhang** 👋


I am currently a Ph.D. student in Engineering Science and Mechanics at [The Pennsylvania State Univeristy](https://www.psu.edu/), where I conduct research on wearable sensors under the supervision of [Prof. Huanyu Cheng](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=YKzDLy0AAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao).


Prior to joining Penn State in 2021, I received a First-class Honours Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electronics and Electrical Engineering from [the Univeristy of Glasgow](https://www.gla.ac.uk/). As part of a joint undergraduate program between the University of Glasgow and [the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)](https://en.uestc.edu.cn/), I earned Bachelor of Engineering degrees from both institutions.
