Outside the lab, I enjoy Chinese classical dance, cultural performances, traveling, and spending time with my two cats, Yorkie and Cessna. These experiences have helped shape my creativity, discipline, and appreciation for diverse perspectives, which I also bring into my research.

## Dance

Dance has been a significant part of my life for more than 20 years. I hold a Level-10 Certificate in Chinese Classical Dance and continue to participate in cultural performances while pursuing my Ph.D.

At Penn State, I have performed in university and community events, including THON and the Multicultural Children's Festival. Through dance, I enjoy sharing Chinese culture and connecting with people from diverse backgrounds.

## Meet Yorkie and Cessna

I share my home with two wonderful cats, Yorkie and Cessna, both adopted from a local shelter.

Yorkie is energetic, curious, and always the first to greet visitors. Cessna is affectionate, vocal, and enjoys following people around the house. They bring a great deal of joy and balance to my life outside of research.

## Let's Connect

One of the most rewarding aspects of academic life is meeting people from diverse cultures and backgrounds. I enjoy learning from others, sharing experiences, and building meaningful connections. If you happen to visit this page, feel free to say hello.
